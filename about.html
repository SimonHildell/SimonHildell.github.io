About Me

Hello!

My name is Simon Hildell. I am a 22 year old guy originally from Eklanda, south of Gothenburg, currently studying third year of architecture at Umeå School of Architecture, UMA.

I enjoy working with different mediums to communicate my ideas in architecure, from complex 3D models to concise and precise sketches. I also really enjoy mathematics which overlaps much with architecture overall, but most so for me in parametric and computational design, my latest obession.

My educational background consists of natural science program (Naturvetenskapsprogrammet) during upper high school (Gymnasiet). I served 15 months in the military as a platoon leader in an artillery company after graduating from gymnasiet. A week after completing my military service i started my architecture studies in Umeå and if everything goes according to plan, I will recieve my bachelor's degree this summer. I have also read Linear Algebra and Calculus 1 on the side of architecture programme because I enjoy mathematics. I have also read Construction CAD to get a deeper understanding of the BIM world and am currently doing the continuation course in Construction CAD, both as extra courses outside the architecture programme.
